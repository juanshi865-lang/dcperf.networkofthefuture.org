About DCperf

The International Workshop on Data Center Performance for Future Network Architectures focuses on performance optimization of data center infrastructures, cloud-edge architectures, and distributed computing systems. Held in conjunction with IEEE ICDCS — a premier conference since 1980 — DCperf addresses critical performance challenges facing modern data centers as they evolve to support AI/ML, edge computing, and global-scale content delivery.

Each edition presents a Best Paper Award selected by the Technical Program Committee based on originality, technical depth, experimental validation, and potential impact on next-generation data center architectures.
