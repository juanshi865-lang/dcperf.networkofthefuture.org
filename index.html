The Workshop About DCperf

The International Workshop on Data Center Performance for Future Network Architectures (DCperf) is a specialized academic workshop focused on the performance optimization of data center infrastructures, cloud-edge network architectures, and distributed computing systems. Held in conjunction with the IEEE International Conference on Distributed Computing Systems (ICDCS) — a premier IEEE conference since 1980 — DCperf addresses the critical performance challenges facing modern data centers as they evolve to support emerging workloads including AI/ML training, edge computing, real-time analytics, and global-scale content delivery.

Each edition of DCperf presents a Best Paper Award to recognize the most outstanding contribution in data center and cloud infrastructure performance research. The award is selected by the Technical Program Committee through rigorous evaluation based on originality, technical depth, experimental validation, and potential for real-world impact on next-generation data center architectures.
