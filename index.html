The Workshop About DCperf

The International Workshop on Data Center Performance for Future Network Architectures (DCperf) focuses on performance optimization of data center and cloud-edge infrastructures. Held with IEEE ICDCS — a premier conference since 1980, technically sponsored by the IEEE Computer Society — DCperf addresses critical challenges facing modern data centers as they evolve to support AI/ML, edge computing, and global-scale content delivery.

Each edition presents a Best Paper Award selected by the Technical Program Committee based on originality, technical depth, experimental validation, and potential impact on next-generation architectures.
