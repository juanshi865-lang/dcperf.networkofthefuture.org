DCperf 2024 Best Paper Award:
Yinan Qian

The Organizing Committee of the International Workshop on Data Center Performance for Future Network Architectures (DCperf 2024) is pleased to announce that Yinan Qian has been selected to receive the DCperf 2024 Best Paper Award for outstanding research in adaptive edge-to-cloud traffic steering for next-generation content delivery networks.

⚡ Best Paper Award — DCperf 2024
Adaptive Edge-to-Cloud Traffic Steering for Latency-Critical Applications in Next-Generation CDN Architectures
Author: Yinan Qian

Award Details

Title Adaptive Edge-to-Cloud Traffic Steering for Latency-Critical Applications in Next-Generation CDN Architectures
Author Yinan Qian (Pinterest, Inc. / Tufts University)
Award Best Paper Award, DCperf 2024
Date 23 July 2024
Workshop DCperf 2024

About the Paper

The paper addresses a fundamental challenge in modern content delivery: dynamically steering traffic across edge-to-cloud continuums to meet strict latency requirements for emerging applications such as real-time video processing, online gaming, and autonomous system coordination. Traditional DNS-based anycast and geo-based routing lack the determinism needed for these latency-sensitive workloads.

Qian proposes an adaptive traffic steering framework that integrates a lightweight latency predictor with a multi-objective optimization engine. The system dynamically routes requests across edge nodes and cloud origins based on real-time network conditions, application-level latency constraints, and edge node capacity. Evaluations using production traffic traces from a global-scale CDN demonstrate consistent latency reductions of 31% at the p99 tail, 24% improvement in request success rate under traffic surge conditions, and 19% reduction in cloud egress costs — all while maintaining computational overhead within practical limits for real-time decision-making.

Selection

Selected by the DCperf 2024 Technical Program Committee based on originality, technical quality, and potential impact on next-generation data center and edge network architectures. The award was presented during DCperf 2024.
