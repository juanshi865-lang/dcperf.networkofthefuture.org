ICDCS Affiliation

DCperf (International Workshop on Data Center Performance for Future Network Architectures) is held in conjunction with the IEEE International Conference on Distributed Computing Systems (ICDCS) — a premier IEEE conference in the field of distributed computing since 1980.

About IEEE ICDCS

The IEEE International Conference on Distributed Computing Systems (ICDCS) is the premier international conference dedicated to the theory, design, implementation, and application of distributed computing systems. Since its founding in 1980, ICDCS has served as the leading forum for researchers and practitioners from academia, industry, and government to present and exchange innovative ideas in distributed systems and related areas.

Technically sponsored by the IEEE Computer Society, ICDCS covers the full spectrum of distributed systems research — from foundational principles and theoretical foundations to practical implementations and real-world deployments. The conference has a long-standing tradition of recognizing outstanding research through its Best Paper Awards and workshop programs.

"ICDCS is the premier international conference dedicated to the theory, design, implementation, and application of distributed computing systems."

— IEEE ICDCS Conference Series

DCperf as an ICDCS Workshop

DCperf is one of the specialized workshops organized under the ICDCS umbrella. As part of ICDCS's workshop program, DCperf benefits from:

  • 45-year legacy — ICDCS's established reputation as the premier IEEE conference on distributed computing, continuously operating since 1980
  • IEEE Computer Society sponsorship — All ICDCS and affiliated workshop publications meet IEEE's rigorous quality standards
  • Publication indexing — Proceedings are available through IEEE Xplore, DBLP, Scopus, and Google Scholar
  • International community — ICDCS draws participants from leading institutions worldwide

Best Paper Award Tradition

ICDCS has a long-standing tradition of recognizing outstanding research through its Best Paper Awards and workshop-level awards. The DCperf Best Paper Award continues this tradition within the specialized domain of data center performance and future network architectures.
