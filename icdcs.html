ICDCS Affiliation

DCperf is held in conjunction with the IEEE International Conference on Distributed Computing Systems (ICDCS) — a premier IEEE conference in the field of distributed computing since 1980.

About IEEE ICDCS

The IEEE International Conference on Distributed Computing Systems (ICDCS) is the premier international conference dedicated to the theory, design, implementation, and application of distributed computing systems. Since its founding in 1980, ICDCS has served as a leading forum for researchers and practitioners from academia, industry, and government. Technically sponsored by the IEEE Computer Society, ICDCS covers the full spectrum of distributed systems research — from foundational principles to practical implementations.

DCperf as an ICDCS Workshop

DCperf is one of the specialized workshops organized under the ICDCS umbrella. As part of ICDCS's workshop program, DCperf benefits from:

  • 45-year legacy — ICDCS's reputation as the premier IEEE conference on distributed computing, continuously operating since 1980
  • IEEE Computer Society sponsorship — All ICDCS and affiliated workshop publications meet IEEE's quality standards
  • Publication indexing — Proceedings available through IEEE Xplore, DBLP, Scopus, and Google Scholar
  • International community — ICDCS draws participants from leading institutions worldwide

Best Paper Award Tradition

ICDCS has a long-standing tradition of recognizing outstanding research through its Best Paper Awards. The DCperf Best Paper Award continues this tradition within the specialized domain of data center performance and future network architectures.

Verification
